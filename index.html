Frequently Asked Questions

We primarily serve Kolkata and its surrounding regions, with strong expertise across key residential and commercial areas of the city.

We have a dedicated legal team that verifies all property documents including title deeds, encumbrance certificates, and approvals from relevant authorities before proceeding with any transaction.

Our fees are competitive and transparent, typically ranging from 1-2% of the property value depending on the service type and property value. We provide a detailed fee structure before starting any engagement.

The timeline varies based on property type and transaction complexity. Typically, residential properties takes 15-20 days, while commercial properties may take 60-90 days including due diligence and documentation.
